What does Zen EE do?

Zen EE allows you to utilize Zencoder, a cloud-based video encoding service from within ExpressionEngine. It uses the power of cloud computing to quickly convert between video formats, all without hammering your server. Learn how it works.

What type of formats can be converted?

Nearly anything can be used as an input. For output we use WebM and MP4 for full HTML5 video browser support.

Why not just use YouTube/Vimeo/Wistia/Viddler/SublimeVideo?

Video hosting services are great in most cases, but a few reasons to self-host include:

  • Full-control over formats and the user interface
  • Privacy, e.g. videos are behind a corporate firewall
  • Centralization: no need to leave the CMS and manage a third-party service

How does it work?

  1. Tell Zen EE where your input videos live. This can be an EE upload directory, any directory on your server, or a public URL)
  2. Tell Zen EE where you'd like the output videos to be placed. Output can be transported via SSH, FTP, or SFTP to your web server, Amazon S3, or Rackspace Cloud Files.
  3. Create an encoding job from the Zen EE admin
  4. Use the Zen EE fieldtype to select your desired video. Reference your video files, thumbnails, and meta information using EE's trademark templating.

  6. PROFIT!

What HTML5 video player(s) can I use?

We use and highly recommend Video.js, which was created by Steve Heffernan of Zencoder.

Here's a few good alternatives:

  • Flowplayer
  • JW Player
  • MediaElement.js
  • Kaltura HTML5 Player
