Why not just use YouTube/Vimeo/Wistia/Viddler/SublimeVideo?

Video hosting services are great in most cases, but a few reasons to self-host include:

  • Control
  • Corporate firewalls
  • Centralization

How does it work?

  1. Tell Zen EE where your input videos live. This can be an EE upload directory, any directory on your server, or a public URL)
  2. Tell Zen EE where you'd like the output videos to be placed. Output can be transported via SSH, FTP, or SFTP to your web server, Amazon S3, or Rackspace Cloud Files.
  3. Create an encoding job

What player(s) can I use?

We've used and recommend Video.js, which was created by Steve Heffernan of Zencoder.

Here's some other great options:

  • Flowplayer
  • JW Player
  • MediaElement.js
  • Kaltura HTML5 Player
